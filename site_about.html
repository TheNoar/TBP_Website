Our Chapter

The Colorado Beta chapter of Tau Beta Pi was founded in 1905 and has since initiated over 4700 members. Colorado Beta has hosted the National Convention twice, in 1951 and 20006. The annual Trick-Or-Can community service project began in 1998 and has been held every Halloween since then. In the past five years, nine Colorado Beta students have received TBP scholarships and one has received a fellowship.

The bilaws for the Colorado Beta chapter can be found on the National Website
