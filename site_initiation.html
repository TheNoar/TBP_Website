Initiation takes place twice each year, in the Fall and Spring.

  • Initiation Checklist:
  • Creativity Presentation
  • Community Service Project
  • Interview with an Officer
  • Polish Your Bent
  • Pay Initiation Fee
  • Attend Initiation!

Creativity Presentation

We like to see what our initiates do outside of school. You will give a 5-minute presentation to the other initiates on something you do or have done creatively outside of school.

Community Service Project

Volunteerism is one of the core values of Tau Beta Pi. All initiates must complete a community service project and submit a 1-paragraph writeup to an officer. The Volunteer Activities that we hold can be found here.

Interview

Each initiate will schedule an interview with a current officer. The officer will ask the initiate questions to gauge whether he or she will be a good fit for Tau Beta Pi.

Bent Polishing

The bent is the symbol of Tau Beta Pi. The shape is the bent of a trestle, which is the load-bearing part of a bridge. It represents Tau Beta Pi's principle of Excellence in Engineering. Each initiate must polish his or her bent.

Initiation Fee

Initiation fees for the 2014-2015 school year are $90. This covers the costs associated with initiation as well as events.

The Initiation Ceremony

The initiation ceremony itself is open only to Tau Beta Pi members and initiates. Family and friends are welcome to attend a reception following the ceremony.
