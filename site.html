Welcome to University of Colorado Boulder Tau Beta Pi

About Us

Tau Beta Pi is a National Honor Society for all engineering majors. We invite exceptional students to join. This typically means Juniors in the top 5% of their class and Seniors in the top 10% of their class.
The CU Boulder chapter was founded in 19XX.
